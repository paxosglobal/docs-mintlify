## Key Features

- Create and manage claim schedules to automate reward payouts
- View all claims associated with your organization
- Update or delete schedules as your payout preferences change
- Claims are processed on-chain to your registered reward addresses

## Claim Schedule vs. Claim

- A **Claim Schedule** defines the rules for when rewards are claimed
- A **Claim** is the record of an individual payout event generated according to a schedule
