---

Paxos uses claim schedules to determine the frequency and timing of reward distributions. A claim represents a single reward payout event triggered according to your configured schedule.

## Key Features

- Create and manage claim schedules to automate reward payouts
- View all claims associated with your organization
- Update or delete schedules as your payout preferences change
- Claims are processed on-chain to your registered reward addresses

## Claim Schedule vs. Claim

- A **Claim Schedule** defines the rules for when rewards are claimed (e.g., monthly, on a specific date)
- A **Claim** is an individual payout event generated according to a schedule
