## **What You'll Do**
1. Open the [Dashboard](https://dashboard.paxos.com): Navigate to the Assets table on the Dashboard landing page.
2. Locate and click on PAXG
3. On the PAXG landing page, you will see available actions: Deposit, Withdraw, Buy, Sell
4. Select and Complete Your Action:
    - Deposit:
      - Select this option to generate deposit instructions for PAXG.
      - Follow the on-screen [Deposit](/guides/dashboard/deposit) instructions to complete your deposit.
    - Withdraw:
      - Select this option to withdraw PAXG to an external destination.
      - Follow the on-screen [Withdraw](/guides/dashboard/withdraw) instructions to complete your withdrawal.
    - Buy:
      - Choose the profile that holds the fiat currency you want to use.
      - Review the current PAXG market price quote.
      - Click Submit to complete your purchase.
    - Sell:
      - Choose the profile containing the PAXG you want to sell.
      - Review the current PAXG market price quote.
      - Click Submit to complete your sale.
5. Unallocated Gold Deposits and Withdrawals are also supported.
    - Unallocated Gold Deposit:
      - On the Unallocated Gold tab, the Deposit action allows you to create Unallocated Gold deposit instructions to convert a deposit of Unallocated Gold into PAXG.
      - You will use these instructions, including the Paxos Wallet Deposit ID when instructing unallocated gold transfers to Paxos.
    - Unallocated Gold Withdrawal:
      - On the Gold Accounts tab, set up your Loco London gold account information.
      - On the Unallocated Gold tab, use the Withdraw action to redeem PAXG from a profile for unallocated gold on a saved gold account.

<Tip>

PAX Gold transactions and price quotes are available during usual London gold market hours (open weekly from 6pm EST on Sunday through 5pm EST on Friday; closed daily between 5-6pm EST time)

</Tip>
