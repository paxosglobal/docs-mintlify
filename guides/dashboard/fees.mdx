## Stablecoin Operations

Stablecoin Operations refers to any combination of deposit, conversion, and transfer between two USD or stablecoin assets.
Withdrawals can be to an external bank account, external digital asset wallet, or to a Paxos-hosted wallet.
To calculate Stablecoin Operations pricing, add together the relevant pricing for the assets you are depositing, minting, burning, and transferring.

| Asset | Deposit | Mint | Burn | Withdraw[\*](#withdraw-note) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Paxos-issued stablecoins<br/>(USDP, PYUSD, USDG, USDL[†](#usdl-restrictions)) | FREE | FREE | FREE | FREE |
| USD | FREE | FREE | FREE | $25 domestic<br/>$35 international |
| USDC | FREE | 0.08% | 0.08% | 0.05%<br/>(max $50 / withdrawal) |

<small id="withdraw-note">
<em>

\* Minimum withdrawal fee of $5.
All withdrawal fees are in addition to the blockchain fee.
Assets can be converted and sent instantly, or held on-platform.
If non-Paxos assets are held on-platform, Custody fees will apply to holdings. Withdrawal fees apply at the time an asset is sent to an external wallet.

</em>
</small>

## Book Transfers

Book Transfers refers to transferring a balance held on-platform at Paxos from one account to another.
You can move asset balances across the Paxos platform with no fee.

## Digital Asset Custody

Custody fees apply only to non-Paxos-issued digital assets.
Custody of Paxos-issued stablecoins including USDP, PYUSD, USDL is free.
