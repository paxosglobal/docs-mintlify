Notifications surface items that need your attention — [pending approvals](/guides/dashboard/admin/approvals), account alerts, and important account events — without leaving your current page. Access them from the **Notifications** item in the Paxos Dashboard sidebar.

## The Notifications Drawer

Click **Notifications** in the sidebar to open the Notifications drawer. The sidebar item displays a badge with the number of unread items, so you can see at a glance when something needs your attention.

Once open, the drawer displays:

- A running count next to the title, showing how many items still need your attention. The count is capped at `50+` so it stays readable.
- Your notifications list, most recent first. Each item shows a title, a short description, and how long ago it arrived (for example, `2 hours ago`).
- Unread notifications are visually highlighted so they are easy to spot at a glance.
- Urgent and warning notifications are color-coded so critical items stand out from routine ones.

## Interact with Notifications

Each notification is designed to get you where you need to go with a single click:

- **Open a notification**: Click anywhere on the notification to jump straight to the relevant page — a pending approval, an account to review, or another action. The notification is automatically marked as read.
- **Dismiss a notification**: Click the ✕ icon to dismiss a notification without navigating away. This marks it as read and removes it from your unread count.
- **Mark all as read**: When you have unread items, use **Mark all as read** in the drawer header to clear everything in one click.

<Tip>
The drawer is keyboard accessible. Use **Tab** to move between notifications and **Enter** or **Space** to open one.
</Tip>

## Real-Time Updates

You do not need to refresh the page to see new activity:

- New notifications appear in the drawer the moment they are created.
- A brief toast message pops up so you are aware even when the drawer is closed. The toast includes a **Review** button to jump directly to the action.
- If a notification is no longer relevant — for example, someone else already approved a request — it updates automatically so you are not left clicking on stale items.

## Account Recovery Prompt

If you have not set up a backup recovery method, a prominent red notification stays pinned at the top of your drawer reminding you to **Set Up Now**.

<Warning>
Setting up account recovery protects you from losing access to your account. We recommend addressing this prompt right away. See [Account Recovery](/guides/dashboard/admin/account-recovery) for setup steps.
</Warning>

## Quick Access to Settings

A gear icon in the drawer header takes you straight to your [Notification Settings](https://dashboard.paxos.com/settings/notifications), so you can adjust how you are notified without hunting through menus.

## Notification Settings

The [Notification Settings](https://dashboard.paxos.com/settings/notifications) page (**Settings** → **Notifications**) lets you control how Paxos reaches you outside the app.

### Email Notifications

The Email Notifications section displays the email address associated with your account and lets you control whether Paxos sends notifications to your inbox.

To manage email notifications:

1. Go to [**Settings** → **Notifications**](https://dashboard.paxos.com/settings/notifications).
2. Use the toggle to turn email notifications **on** or **off**.

Changes save automatically. You will see a confirmation once applied.

<Info>
If something goes wrong while saving, you will be notified and your previous setting is preserved.
</Info>

<Note>
Disabling email notifications does not hide in-app notifications — you will still see them in the drawer. This setting only controls whether Paxos also sends them to your inbox.
</Note>
