---
title: 'Deposit Addresses'
description: Access saved crypto deposit addresses for receiving digital assets into your Paxos profile.
---

![Addresses Deposit Instructions](/images/address-deposit-instructions.png)

Use [Crypto Addresses](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/addresses) to access saved crypto deposit addresses.

- **Deposit**: The asset (Source) is deposited directly into the selected Paxos Profile (Destination).

<Tip>

Deposit addresses are immutable. If your deposit requirements change, create a new set of addresses.

</Tip>

To download deposit details, select the **Actions** menu and use **Export Instructions**.
