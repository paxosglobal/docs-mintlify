---
title: 'Convert'
description: Convert between USD and stablecoins within your Paxos profile.
---


Go to the [Dashboard](https://dashboard.paxos.com/) and click **Convert** in the top-right corner to convert between USD and stablecoins. Converted assets are held in your Paxos [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile).

## Mint

Convert your USD balance to a stablecoin token (USD → Stablecoin).

1. Select **Mint**.
1. Choose the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) that holds the USD balance.
1. Select the stablecoin to mint (for example, USDG, PYUSD, or USDP).
1. Enter the **Amount** to convert.
1. Review the details and confirm.

## Redeem

Convert your stablecoin tokens back to USD (Stablecoin → USD).

1. Select **Redeem**.
1. Choose the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) that holds the stablecoin balance.
1. Select the stablecoin to redeem.
1. Enter the **Amount** to convert.
1. Review the details and confirm.

## Swap

Exchange one stablecoin for another (Stablecoin → Stablecoin).

1. Select **Swap**.
1. Choose the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) that holds the stablecoin you want to swap from.
1. Select the stablecoin to receive.
1. Enter the **Amount** to swap.
1. Review the details and confirm.

Go to [Transactions > Asset Movements](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/transactions/asset-movements) or [Transactions > Conversions](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/transactions/conversions) to monitor activity.

> Want to automatically convert incoming deposits? [Set up an Automation](/guides/dashboard/automate).

> Can't find the right conversion options?
> The desired option may not be supported by the [Entity](/guides/dashboard/organization#entities) or by the Paxos platform.
