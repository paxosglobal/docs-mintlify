---
title: 'Convert'
description: Convert between USD and stablecoins, and send the result to a profile or external account.
---


Go to the [Dashboard](https://dashboard.paxos.com/) and click **Convert** in the top-right corner to convert between USD and stablecoins.

## Mint

Convert your USD balance to a stablecoin token (USD → Stablecoin).

1. Select **Mint**.
1. Choose the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) that holds the USD balance.
1. Select the stablecoin to mint (for example, USDG, PYUSD, or USDP).
1. Choose the destination: **Paxos Profile** or **External Account**. If sending to an external account, select the network and wallet address.
1. Enter the **Amount** to convert.
1. Review the details and confirm.

![Mint Stablecoin](/images/convert-mint.png)

## Redeem

Convert your stablecoin tokens back to USD (Stablecoin → USD).

1. Select **Redeem**.
1. Choose the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) that holds the stablecoin balance.
1. Select the stablecoin to redeem.
1. Choose the destination: **Paxos Profile** or **External Account**. If sending to an external account, select the network and saved account.
1. Enter the **Amount** to convert.
1. Review the details and confirm.

![Redeem Stablecoin](/images/convert-redeem.png)

## Swap

Exchange one stablecoin for another (Stablecoin → Stablecoin).

1. Select **Swap**.
1. Choose the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) that holds the stablecoin you want to swap from.
1. Select the stablecoin to receive.
1. Choose the destination: **Paxos Profile** or **External Account**. If sending to an external account, select the network and wallet address.
1. Enter the **Amount** to swap.
1. Review the details and confirm.

![Swap Stablecoin](/images/convert-swap.png)

Go to [Transactions > Conversions](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/transactions/conversions) to monitor conversion activity.
