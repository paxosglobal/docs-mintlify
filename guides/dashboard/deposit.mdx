---
title: 'Deposit'
description: Send cash or crypto to the Paxos platform.
---


Go to the [Dashboard](https://dashboard.paxos.com/) and click **Deposit** in the top-right corner to generate deposit instructions (for USD) or deposit addresses (for crypto) for sending funds to the Paxos platform.

1. Select the **Asset** and corresponding crypto or fiat **Network** for the deposit. For bank wire deposits, specify the routing type.
1. Choose the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) where the assets will be deposited on the Paxos platform.
1. Off the platform, use the generated instruction or address to send the assets to the Paxos platform.

![Deposit Details](/images/deposit-details.png)

Go to [Transactions > Deposits & Transfers](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/transactions/deposits-transfers) to monitor deposit activity.

> Previously generated instructions and addresses are available in [Deposit Addresses](/guides/dashboard/deposit-addresses) (crypto) and [Deposit Instructions](/guides/dashboard/fiat-deposit-instructions) (fiat).
