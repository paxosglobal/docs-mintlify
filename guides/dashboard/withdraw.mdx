---
title: 'Withdraw'
description: Send funds from your Paxos account to an external destination or another Profile or Entity.
---


Go to the [Dashboard](https://dashboard.paxos.com/) and click **Withdraw** in the top-right corner to send funds off the Paxos platform or move them between Profiles and Entities.

![Withdraw Options](/images/withdraw-options.png)

## Crypto to an External Address

Move crypto assets to an external address outside of Paxos.

1. Select **Crypto to an External Address**.
1. Choose the **Asset** and select the **Network** for the withdrawal.
1. Select the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) where the assets will be _withdrawn from_ (the source).
1. Choose a saved address where the asset will be sent.<br />Optional: [Create a new network address](/guides/dashboard/withdrawal-addresses).
1. Enter the **Amount** to withdraw.<br />Optional: Add a note for the withdrawal.

![Crypto Withdrawal](/images/withdraw-crypto.png)

Go to [Transactions > Asset Movements](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/transactions/asset-movements) to monitor activity.

## USD to a Bank Account

Withdraw USD to an external bank account.

1. Select **USD to a Bank Account**.
1. Select the [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) where the USD will be _withdrawn from_ (the source).
1. Choose a saved fiat account where the USD will be sent.<br />Optional: [Add a fiat account](/guides/dashboard/fiat).
1. Enter the **Amount** to withdraw.<br />Optional: Add a note for the withdrawal.

![Fiat Withdrawal](/images/withdraw-fiat.png)

Go to [Transactions > Asset Movements](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/transactions/asset-movements) to monitor activity.

### Approvals for fiat withdrawals

If your organization has [Approvals](/guides/dashboard/admin/approvals) enabled for fiat withdrawals, external fiat withdrawals require review and approval from another team member before they can be completed.

## To a Different Paxos Profile

Move stablecoins or other assets to another Profile within the same Entity.

Ensure the destination [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) already exists before beginning the withdrawal.

1. Select **To a different Paxos Profile**.
1. Choose the **Asset**.
1. Select the Profile where the assets will be _withdrawn from_ (the source).
1. Choose the Profile where the assets will be sent (the destination).
1. Enter the **Amount** to withdraw.<br />Optional: Add a note for the withdrawal.

![Transfer to Profile](/images/withdraw-profile.png)

Go to [Transactions > Asset Movements](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/transactions/asset-movements) to monitor activity.

## To a Different Paxos Entity

Move assets between two Entities belonging to the same Organization or to a different Organization on the Paxos platform.

> Withdrawing USD between Entities is prohibited in some jurisdictions.
> Contact [Support](https://support.paxos.com) if you run into any problems with this restriction.

Ensure the destination [Profile](/guides/dashboard/organization#profile) already exists before beginning the withdrawal.
This may require contacting someone outside your Organization to get the destination Profile ID.

1. Select **To a different Paxos Entity**.
1. Choose the **Asset**.
> If an asset is not listed, it is not available for withdrawal.
1. Select the Profile where the assets will be _withdrawn from_ (the source).
1. Enter the Profile ID where the assets will be sent (the destination).
1. Enter the **Amount** to withdraw.<br />Optional: Add a note for the withdrawal.

![Transfer to Entity](/images/withdraw-entity.png)

Go to [Transactions > Asset Movements](https://dashboard.paxos.com/dashboard/transactions/asset-movements) to monitor activity.

> When contacting [Support](https://support.paxos.com) about a withdrawal, include the unique **Transfer ID** and **Group ID** in your message to help our agents find the correct transaction.
