FIX (Financial Information eXchange) is an electronic messaging protocol widely adopted by financial institutions to transmit trading activity such as submitting or canceling orders and receiving execution information. The [FIX API][fix-api] is based on FIX 4.2 and modeled after common Forex FIX implementations.
