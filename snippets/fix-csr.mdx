Generate the certificate signing request (CSR) following your internal key management best practices. 
Make sure to use the **RSA key algorithm**.
Paxos recommends a **key length of 4096 bits**. 

As a secondary security measure, a Paxos Support will contact you to verify its content.
Please include the following technical contact information:

- Name
- Email
- Phone

Send the CSR and technical contact information to support@paxos.com.

Paxos will follow up with next steps.
