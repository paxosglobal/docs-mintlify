To move USD off the Platform, first use [Create Fiat Account][create-fiat-account] to let Paxos know where to transfer the funds.
Then, use the returned Fiat Account `id` with [Create Fiat Withdrawal][create-fiat-withdrawal] to transfer funds to the external account.
